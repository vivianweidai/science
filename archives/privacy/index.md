# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Science app and vivianweidai.com ("we", "our") handle your information.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The Science app and website:

- Do not require account creation or login
- Do not collect analytics or usage data
- Do not use cookies or tracking technologies
- Do not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or location

## Data Sources

All content displayed in the app is fetched from publicly available files hosted on GitHub. No user data is sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

The app loads content from GitHub's raw content servers. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to those requests. We do not integrate any other third-party services, advertising, or analytics.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at [privacy@vivianweidai.com](mailto:privacy@vivianweidai.com).
