# Fungi

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| fungi | decompose by absorption | dominant haploid form |
| fungi | hyphae chitin cell walls | prevent hypotonic bursting |
| fungi | septa divide hyphae cells | large pores for organelles |
| coenocytic | no septa divisions | continuous cytoplasm lots nuclei |
| coenocytic | repeated cell division | with no cytokinesis |
| mycorrhizae | symbiosis with plants | trade minerals for sugar |
| mycorrhizae | vital for all vascular plants | wrap roots or insert into cells |
| asexual reproduction | quickly conquer food source | leave food grows mold |
| asexual reproduction | haploids reproduce by mitosis | or by pinching bud cells |
| sexual reproduction | when encounter partner | meet and join by pheromones |
| sexual reproduction | plasmogamy cytoplasm union | delayed union of nuclei |
| sexual reproduction | karyogamy fertilize zygote | meiosis restores haploids |
