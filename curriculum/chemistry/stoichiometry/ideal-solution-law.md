# Ideal Solution Law

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| vapor pressure | equilibrium gas pressure | of evaporation and condensation |
| vapor pressure | non volatile solute | has no evaporation |
| vapor pressure | when replaces part of solution | decreases vapor pressure |
| ideal solution law | total pressure | proportional to solvent fraction |
| ideal solution law | $P = \chi_{\text{solvent}}P_{\text{solvent}}$ | ratio with nonvolatile solute |
| ideal solution law | mixture of solvents | mixture of partial pressures |
| ideal solution law | $P_{T} = = \chi_{A}P_{A} + \chi_{B}P_{B}$ | by partial pressure |
