# Energy

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| potential energy | $U = \frac{1}{2}kx^{2}$ | integration |
| potential energy | $U = \frac{1}{2}kA^{2}\cos^{2}\omega t$ | expansion |
| kinetic energy | $v(t) = - A\omega\sin\omega t$ | differentiation |
| kinetic energy | $K = \frac{1}{2}kA^{2}\sin^{2}\omega t$ | substitution |
| mechanical energy | $E = U + K$ | definition |
| mechanical energy | $E = \frac{1}{2}kA^{2}$ | trigonometry |
| velocity | $v^{2} = \frac{k}{m}\left( A^{2} - x^{2} \right)$ | rearrangement |
