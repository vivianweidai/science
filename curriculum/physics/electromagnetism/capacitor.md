# Capacitor

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| capacitor | store potential energy | of charge separation |
| capacitor | $C = \frac{Q}{V}$ | charge per potential difference |
| parallel plate | archetype capacitor | equal opposite charges |
| parallel plate | dielectric insulator | lower field higher capacity |
| parallel plate | $E = \frac{\sigma}{\epsilon_{0}}$ | $C = \kappa\epsilon_{0}\frac{A}{d}$ |
| parallel plate | $C = C_{1} + C_{2}$ | parallel |
| parallel plate | $\frac{1}{C} = \frac{1}{C_{1}} + \frac{1}{C_{2}}$ | series |
| energy | energy stored | work to create configuration |
| energy | $U = \frac{1}{2}CV^{2}$ | stored energy |
| energy | $u_{E} = \frac{1}{2}\epsilon_{0}E^{2}$ | energy density |
