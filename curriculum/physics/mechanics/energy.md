# Energy

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| energy | capacity to do work | conserved in universe |
| energy | $W = \mathbf{F} \cdot \mathbf{x}$ | force over distance |
| energy | $W = \int m\mathbf{v}dv$ | work energy |
| energy | $K = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2}$ | kinetic energy |
| conservative | force performs work | stored as potential energy |
| conservative | $W = \oint\mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = 0$ | path independent state variable |
| potential | energy of configuration | by conservative force |
| potential | $U = - \int\mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r}$ | negative work stored |
| potential | $F = - \frac{dU}{dx}$ | restores to equilibrium |
| power | $\overline{P} = \frac{\Delta W}{\Delta t}$ | rate of energy over time |
| power | $P = \mathbf{F} \cdot \mathbf{v}$ | substitution |
