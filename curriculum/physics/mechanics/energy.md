# Energy

<table>
<tr><th>Label</th><th>Formula / Concept</th><th>Description</th></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>energy</td><td>capacity to do work</td><td>conserved in universe</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>energy</td><td>$W = \mathbf{F} \cdot \mathbf{x}$</td><td>force over distance</td></tr>
<tr><td>energy</td><td>$W = \int m\mathbf{v}dv$</td><td>work energy</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>energy</td><td>$K = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2}$</td><td>kinetic energy</td></tr>
<tr><td>conservative</td><td>force performs work</td><td>stored as potential energy</td></tr>
<tr><td>conservative</td><td>$W = \oint\mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = 0$</td><td>path independent state variable</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>potential</td><td>energy of configuration</td><td>by conservative force</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>potential</td><td>$U = - \int\mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r}$</td><td>negative work stored</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>potential</td><td>$F = - \frac{dU}{dx}$</td><td>restores to equilibrium</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>power</td><td>$\overline{P} = \frac{\Delta W}{\Delta t}$</td><td>rate of energy over time</td></tr>
<tr class="highlight"><td>power</td><td>$P = \mathbf{F} \cdot \mathbf{v}$</td><td>substitution</td></tr>
</table>
