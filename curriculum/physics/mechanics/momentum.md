# Momentum

| Label | Formula / Concept | Description |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| momentum | $\mathbf{p} = m\mathbf{v}$ | quantity of inertia |
| momentum | $\mathbf{F} = \frac{d\mathbf{p}}{dt}$ | second law |
| momentum | $P = \sum m_{i}v_{i} = \frac{d}{dt}\sum m_{i}x_{i}$ | system of particles |
| momentum | $P = Mv_{cm}$ | center of mass |
| momentum | $F_{ext} = \frac{dP}{dt} = 0$ | conservation |
